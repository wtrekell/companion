## About Syntax & Empathy

Syntax & Empathy is a space dedicated to exploring the intersection of technical precision and human-centered design. This companion repository serves as a collection of artifacts, insights, and experiments from my work with AI and various applications.

## What You'll Find Here

- **Articles**: Thoughts on design leadership, Git workflows, and AI collaboration
- **Projects**: Creative experiments and project documentation
- **Resources**: Useful tools, templates, and references

## Philosophy

The name "Syntax & Empathy" reflects a belief that the best work happens at the intersection of technical excellence and deep human understanding. Whether it's code, design, or documentation, both precision and care matter.

## Repository Structure

- `00-resources/`: Tools and reference materials
- `01-archive/`: Historical content and archives
- `02-articles/`: Long-form articles and essays
- `03-projects/`: Project documentation
- `04-and-so-on/`: Additional content
