## Systems Design for Human-AI Collaboration

Syntax & Empathy focuses on structured frameworks and workflow optimizations for Product, Service, and User Experience designers using ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and other LLMs. The work overlaps with documentation, product management, research, and writing.

Prompt Engineering Building Blocks help you keep AI conversations on task and recover when human-to-machine translation falls apart. You'll understand why certain approaches consistently work while others fail.

Systematic Workflow Integrations provide pattern libraries for common UX tasks and templates you can adapt to your process. Approaches for building what works across teams and projects.
