# Assets for "Algorithmic Theatre"

This directory contains all visual assets, AI interaction logs, and supplementary graphics that support the article "[Algorithmic Theatre](/02-articles/251109-on-authenticity/0927-algorithmic-theatre/)".

## Summary

These assets serve as integral visual and data-driven companions to the "[Algorithmic Theatre](/02-articles/251109-on-authenticity/0927-algorithmic-theatre/)" article. They include screenshots of AI assistant interactions, process diagrams illustrating design and AI workflows, and promotional images. Their purpose is to enhance the reader's understanding by providing direct visual evidence and illustrative context for the article's exploration of AI collaboration, authenticity, and the performative aspects of AI outputs.

## About These Assets

These files are organized to provide a comprehensive visual and documentary backdrop for the "Algorithmic Theatre" article. They directly illustrate the methodologies and findings discussed, particularly concerning:
- **Documenting AI Interactions:** HTML logs and corresponding screenshots (`claude-*.html`, `claude-*.png`) offer transparent records of specific prompts and AI responses, demonstrating the iterative nature of AI collaboration.
- **Visualizing Complex Processes:** Diagrams (`process-*.png`, `AI Writing Process Tracker - [].png`) break down abstract concepts and workflows into digestible visual representations.
- **Article Presentation:** Dedicated banner and social media images (`part-02-banner.png`, `part-02-social.png`) ensure consistent branding and effective dissemination of the article's content.
